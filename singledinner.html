Chapathi Koorma

About Chapathi Koorma

Chapathi Koorma is a classic Indian dish combining soft and fluffy chapathis with a rich and flavorful koorma, a mildly spiced curry made with vegetables or meat. It is a perfect meal for lunch or dinner, offering a satisfying and healthy combination of textures and flavors.

Cooking Type

The chapathis are prepared by rolling whole wheat dough into thin flatbreads and cooking them on a tawa (griddle) with minimal oil. The koorma is made by slow-cooking a mixture of vegetables or meat with aromatic spices and a creamy base, typically made from coconut, yogurt, or cashew paste.

A Small History

Chapathi Koorma has its roots in traditional Indian cuisine, particularly from the southern regions of India. While chapathis are a staple across the country, koorma has historically been a royal dish, known for its rich flavor and creamy texture. Over time, this combination became popular in many Indian households.
