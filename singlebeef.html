Beef Stroganoff

About the Dish

Beef Stroganoff is a classic Russian dish made of sautéed beef in a rich sour cream sauce. It's often served over noodles or rice, providing a hearty and flavorful meal.

Cooking Type

Beef Stroganoff is typically made by pan-searing the beef and then simmering it with onions, mushrooms, and a sour cream-based sauce until it's tender and flavorful.

History

This dish is believed to have been named after a member of the Stroganov family, one of Russia’s wealthiest families, and has been enjoyed since the 19th century. It's now popular worldwide.
